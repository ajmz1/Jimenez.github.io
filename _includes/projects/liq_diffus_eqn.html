3 simple equations to govern the physics of fluid flow through porous media.

Sure, oil price is important. However, first you have to produce it from 1000s of feet below surface. This was one of my first fun programming projects, a visual experiment of simulating gravity in a system with hundreds of interacting planets. The calculations are done with simple physics formulas, with a bit of tweaking and damping to make them more robust. Clicking adds an invisible mass that makes all objects move towards the cursor. Everything is rendered on a <canvas> element, and it's quite satisfying to move the colorful balls around!
